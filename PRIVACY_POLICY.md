# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: December 24, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our application ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Information

#### a. Blockchain Wallet Information
- When you connect your Web3 wallet, we may access your public wallet address
- We do NOT have access to your private keys or seed phrases
- Blockchain transactions are public by nature and permanently recorded on-chain

#### b. Usage Data
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- IP address (anonymized)
- Pages visited and time spent
- Interaction with features

#### c. YouTube Data
- We fetch public video information from YouTube channels
- This includes video titles, descriptions, thumbnails, and view counts
- All data is publicly available through YouTube's API

### 2. Information You Provide

- None required - our app does not require user registration
- If you connect a wallet, you're only providing your public address

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Service Operation**: To provide and maintain the app functionality
2. **Security**: To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
3. **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our app (aggregated, anonymized data)
4. **Improvements**: To enhance user experience and fix bugs

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- All API keys and sensitive credentials are encrypted
- No personal data is stored in our databases
- Wallet connections are handled through secure, industry-standard protocols (WalletConnect)
- All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. **YouTube Data API v3**
   - Purpose: Fetch public video information
   - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. **WalletConnect**
   - Purpose: Secure wallet connection
   - Privacy Policy: https://walletconnect.com/privacy

3. **Polygon Network**
   - Purpose: Blockchain interactions
   - Privacy Policy: https://polygon.technology/privacy-policy

4. **Vercel (Hosting)**
   - Purpose: Application hosting and delivery
   - Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

## Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage for:
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Temporary caching to improve performance
- User preferences (if applicable)

No cookies are used for tracking or advertising purposes.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### GDPR (European Union)
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

### CCPA (California)
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (we do NOT sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- Clear browser local storage to remove all stored data
- Disconnect your wallet to revoke access
- Contact us at: privacy@yourdomain.com

## Data Retention

- **Wallet connections**: Temporary, session-based only
- **Usage logs**: Anonymized and retained for 90 days
- **Error logs**: Sanitized and retained for 30 days
